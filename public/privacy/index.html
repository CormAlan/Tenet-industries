Privacy Policy

Who we are

Tenet Industries is a defense technology company based in Stockholm, Sweden. We build low-cost, mass-producible defense systems. You can reach us at founders@tenetindustries.com.

What data we collect

Contact form. When you submit the contact form on this website, we collect the following information you provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company or organization
  • Subject
  • Message

This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and route it to the appropriate person at Tenet Industries.

Server and analytics data. This website is hosted on Vercel. When you visit, Vercel may log your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and request timestamps as part of standard server operation. We use Vercel Web Analytics, which collects aggregated, anonymized traffic data. No personally identifiable information is stored by Vercel Analytics.

Legal basis (GDPR)

For contact form submissions, our legal basis is Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR — processing necessary to respond to your request.

For analytics and server logs, our legal basis is Article 6(1)(f) — our legitimate interest in understanding how the site is used and maintaining its security.

Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as necessary to handle your inquiry and any resulting business relationship, and no longer than two years unless a longer retention period is required. Vercel server logs and analytics data are retained according to Vercel's privacy policy.

Third parties

We use Vercel for website hosting and analytics. Vercel processes data on our behalf subject to their privacy policy. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any other third parties.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY) at imy.se

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at founders@tenetindustries.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
